About Nikola Jokić

Nikola Jokić is a Serbian professional basketball player born on February 19, 1995, in Sombor, Serbia. He currently plays as a center for the Denver Nuggets in the NBA.

Jokić was selected by the Denver Nuggets with the 41st overall pick in the 2014 NBA Draft. He has since become one of the league's most skilled and versatile big men, known for his exceptional passing, scoring ability, and basketball IQ.

Accolades

  • NBA Most Valuable Player (2021)
  • NBA All-Star (2019, 2020, 2021)
  • All-NBA First Team (2021)
  • NBA All-Rookie First Team (2016)
